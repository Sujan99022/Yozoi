    Cleaning

    Our service providers can clean all in-door areas of your home including vacuuming, mopping, dusting, and polishing. Outdoor cleaning services are also available including patios, driveways, and gardens.

    Footcare

    Cutting nails, hydrating, and massaging feet for improved circulation.

    Gardening

    All routine garden maintenance work such as cutting grass, trimming hedges and plants, and clearing garden waste.

    Haircare

    Full barber services for men and hair saloon services for ladies in the comfort of your own home.

    Handyperson

    Routine home maintenance such as changing light bulbs, batteries, and fuses. Installing fixture, mobility equipment, goods such as new fridges, toasters, kettle and so on. Please specify as accurately as possible in your service request.

    Laundry

    Our service providers can do all your laundry either in your own home or take it away for laundering and delivery back to you. Please specify in your service request.

    Mobility assistance

    We can provide transport and accompany you to appointments such as visits to the doctor, dentist or pharmacy as well as visits to cafes, restaurants and community events.

    Pets

    Feeding pets, walking dogs and visits to the vet.

    Preparing meals

    Do you need a nutritional plan to optimise health or need a service to prepare and serve your meals. Yozoi service provider can help make a nutritional plan, cook meals in you home daily or as and when you need, serve you food and tidy up kitchen. Alternatively, home cooked meals can be delivered and served to you at home. Contact us to assess and specify your needs.

    Shopping assistance

    We can provide transport and accompany you on shopping trips to the supermarkets or other outlets. Alternatively, our service providers can do the shopping for you, deliver to your home and store in your home to fit with your requirements.

    Security assistance

    Do you need a trusted person to visit to check around and in your house or be present whilst you use the bathroom in case you have an accident? Yozoi can provide such assistance 24/7 and every day of the year whenever you need it.
